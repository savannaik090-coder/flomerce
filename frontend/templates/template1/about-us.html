About Royal Meenakari

450 years of legacy in Jewellery - Gold | Polki | Jadau | Meenakari | uncut - National Award Winner - Jasthan Meenakar

Royal Meenakari Jewellers

450 Years of Heritage & Excellence

At Royal Meenakari, we carry forward a legacy of 450 years in the art of jewellery making. Based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we are proud National Award Winners specializing in Gold, Polki, Jadau, Meenakari, and uncut diamonds.

Our commitment to authentic craftsmanship and traditional artistry has been passed down through generations, making us one of the most trusted names in heritage jewellery.

Every piece in our collection reflects centuries of expertise, artistic brilliance, and the timeless beauty of traditional Indian jewellery, crafted to perfection for discerning connoisseurs worldwide.

Our Mission

Royal Meenakari is more than just a jewellery brand – it is a custodian of 450 years of traditional Indian jewellery artistry, keeping heritage craft alive for future generations.

We aim to preserve and promote the finest traditions of Gold, Polki, Jadau, and Meenakari work, creating masterpieces that blend timeless elegance with contemporary appeal.

Our commitment extends beyond creating beautiful jewellery – we are dedicated to supporting traditional artisans, preserving ancient meenakari techniques, and ensuring that this priceless heritage continues to shine for centuries to come.
